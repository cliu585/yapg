Donations

Young Artists’ Performance Group is commited towards providing support towards the centers and facilities that we are affiliated with and volunteer for. Below includes some of our work towards providing assistance to various centers and facilities.

August 22nd, 2020

Safety and sanitation became an increasingly important issue as a result of the COVID-19 global pandemic, and YAPG pledged to help provide a positive impact for the facilities that the group has performed for. Young Artists’ Performance Group members donated personal protective equipment including masks and face shields as well as cleaning & sanitation supplies to the Massapequa Adult Day Care Center.
